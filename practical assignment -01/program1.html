( rungta international skills university )

Rungta International Skills University (RIU) is a private university in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, that was established in 2025 after the Rungta R-1 Engineering College was reorganized. It is now a university that offers industry-immersive degree programs designed with skills-based learning, incorporating elements like design thinking, entrepreneurship, and data science into its curriculum. The university has a large campus, a focus on internships and global alliances, and has been collaborating with companies like Google and Microsoft on curriculum development. Key features Establishment: It was established in 2025 following the reorganization of the Rungta R-1 Engineering College. Location: It is located in Bhilai, Chhattisgarh. Curriculum: The university offers industry-immersive degree programs with a focus on skills-based learning and holistic development in line with the NEP 2020 framework. It blends technical knowledge with cross-functional areas like design thinking, law, medicine, business, entrepreneurship, and behavioral sciences, often termed the STEAM model (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics). Industry collaboration: The curriculum is developed with input from industry experts, and the university has a large number of internship partnerships and global academic alliances. Campus: It has a state-of-the-art campus spread over 80+ acres with over 1.5 million square feet of built-up space. Admissions: Admissions for 2025 were open, and the application process may involve online counseling conducted by the Government of Chhattisgarh.
